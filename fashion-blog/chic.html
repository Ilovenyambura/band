My idea of fashion

To begin with,la mode is a french word that means fashion.

For me fashion is about what looks good on a person.Designer names do not mean much if you watch fashion shows from Paris,Madrid etc etc if you'd be caught dead wearing the designer clothes.

To be honest,I also wouldn't be caught dead wearing those designer clothes.

I mean,they are either too scanty or too humongous.Just not clothes I'd enjoy wearing

So just to clear the air,I'll only discuss clothes I am very comfortable seeing myself in.However,anyone with more ideas on clothes or jewellery i can discuss can message me

Chambray

I know most of you are wondering what piece of clothing a chambray is,but worry no more!A chambray is what we Kenyans commonly call a denim shirt.

I have been in love with denim since the trend began way back when i was just a very young girl

First,when buying a chambray please do not buy one with heavy fabric because it is ususally worn on top of clothe like crop tops,sleevless tops or [for the boys/men]as a shirt.

I honestly think it's great when the weather seems mixed i.e when you are unsure about whether it is going to be sunny or rainy

Also,I wouldn't advise anyone to buy a tight chambray.It is supposed to allow your skin to breathe not suffocate.

Chambrays are not sold in blue only,for those who feel like blue denim is not their cup of tea.They are available in black,maroon,green.It just depends on the seller.

To wrap it up,chambrays do not cost more than 800KES.Most are sold at Toy market,Gikomba market and Ngara
